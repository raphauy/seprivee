# SePrivée

Website for **SePrivée** - Private Dining & Curated Events studio based in Montevideo and Maldonado, Uruguay.

## About

SePrivée designs bespoke culinary experiences in private settings. From intimate dinners to curated events, sushi experiences, and cooking classes - bringing the restaurant experience to your home.

## Pages

- **Home** - Introduction and services overview
- **Experiences** - Detailed service offerings (Private Dining, Curated Events, Sushi, Cooking Classes)
- **Menus** - Sample menus and culinary gallery
- **About** - Story, values, and FAQ
- **Contact** - Inquiry form and direct contact

## Features

- Bilingual (Spanish/English)
- Responsive design
- Contact form with email notifications
